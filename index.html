Our Philosophy

Providing the best massage and therapy possible off site or at home office. In a world full of stress, I want to provide a means for the body to rest and heal.

Menu of Services

Swedish

A classic massage technique that relaxes tension in the body and promotes circulation.

Deep Tissue

Alleviates sore muscles and eases tension by going beyond the usual superficial muscle massage with pinching and pressing at acupressure points.

Sports Massage

Combines both physical and energetic aspects. Pre event, post event, maintenance, or rehabilitative.

Myofascial

Stimulate the lymphatic system to expel the toxins out from the body and reduce adhesions.

Grastons

Metal instruments used to help stretch and loosen tissue.

About Me

I am an Athletic Trainer at a local High School and a massage therapist. I love working with people and I strive to make them better. Soft tissue manipulation, aka massage, is a wonderful tool to help anyone feel better! Pair that with stretching and proper self care and its an undefeatable team. I have a passion for everything I do and I love to keep learning! Family and friends are so important to me, so I enjoy a comfortable homey environment. When you come in for an appointment, it will be centered and focused on you.
